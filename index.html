Our Heritage

Baked with Love
in the Heart of Akureyri

Nestled in the charming streets of Akureyri, Axelsbakarí has been crafting Iceland's finest pastries, breads, and cinnamon rolls for over two decades. Every morning, our bakers rise before dawn to prepare fresh, hand-shaped goods using time-honored Nordic recipes and locally sourced ingredients.

From flaky croissants to our legendary kleinuhringir, every bite tells a story of patience, craft, and the warm Icelandic spirit that makes our bakery a beloved gathering place.
